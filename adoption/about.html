About Oregon Paws Shelter

Founded in 2010, Oregon Paws Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals in Portland, Oregon. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for pets in need and match them with loving families.

We care for over 500 animals annually, providing medical care, training, and love. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff work tirelessly to ensure every pet finds a forever home.
