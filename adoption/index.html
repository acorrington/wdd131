Welcome to Oregon Paws Shelter

We are dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need. Our shelter in Portland, Oregon, cares for dogs, cats, and more, ensuring they are healthy and ready for adoption. Join us in making a difference!

Why Choose Oregon Paws Shelter?

Our Mission

  • Rescue and rehabilitate animals in need across Portland.
  • Find loving, forever homes for every pet in our care.
  • Educate the community on responsible pet ownership.

Our Services

  • Comprehensive medical care, including vaccinations and spay/neuter.
  • Behavioral training to prepare pets for their new homes.
  • Fostering programs and adoption events to connect pets with families.

The Adoption Journey

Adoption Process

  • Submit an application and meet your potential pet.
  • Complete a home check to ensure a safe environment.
  • Finalize the adoption and welcome your new family member!

What to Expect

  • Applications are reviewed within 3-5 business days.
  • We provide follow-up support to ensure a smooth transition.
  • Access to resources and training tips post-adoption.
